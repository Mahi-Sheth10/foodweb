Summer Vibes

Mint Salad

Juicy watermelon cubes tossed with crumbled feta, fresh mint, a drizzle of honey, and lime juice.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup chopped cucumber
  • ¼ cup fresh mint leaves
  • ½ cup plain yogurt
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Mix yogurt, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  2. Stir in cucumber and chopped mint.
  3. Chill and serve cold.
Mango Yogurt

Layers of chilled Greek yogurt, mango puree, granola, and fresh fruit!!

Ingredients:

  • 1 ripe mango (chopped)
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 1 tsp honey or sugar
  • Optional: cardamom powder

Steps:

  1. Blend mango and yogurt until smooth.
  2. Add honey and optional cardamom.
  3. Chill and serve as a dessert or snack.
Raita

Grated cucumber mixed with chilled curd, cumin powder, mint, salt, and a hint of black salt

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • ½ cup grated cucumber or chopped veggies
  • 1 tsp roasted cumin powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Optional: chopped coriander

Steps:

  1. Whisk yogurt until smooth.
  2. Add vegetables, salt, and cumin powder.
  3. Mix well and serve chilled.
Rice & Curry

Soft rice mixed with chilled curd, tempered with mustard seeds, green chilies, and curry leaves, topped with pomegranate.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 1 cup mixed vegetable curry or dal
  • Spices (turmeric, chili, cumin)
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp oil or ghee

Steps:

  1. Prepare curry with vegetables and spices.
  2. Serve hot with steamed rice.
  3. Optional: add pickle or salad on the side.
Tacos

Mini tortillas with grilled paneer cubes, sautéed sweet corn, onions, and coriander chutney

Ingredients:

  • 4 taco shells
  • 1 cup mixed veggies
  • ½ cup cooked beans
  • Salsa or tomato chutney
  • Optional: cheese, lettuce

Steps:

  1. Fill taco shells with beans and veggies.
  2. Add salsa and toppings.
  3. Serve immediately for crunchiness.
Rose Lemonade

Rose Lemonade is a refreshing summer drink made by mixing rose syrup (like Rooh Afza), fresh lemon juice, and chilled soda or water

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cold water
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp rose water
  • 1 tbsp sugar or honey
  • Ice cubes

Steps:

  1. Mix lemon juice, rose water, and sweetener in water.
  2. Add ice and stir well.
  3. Serve chilled and enjoy.
