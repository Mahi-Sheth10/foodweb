Turmeric Milk

A warm and healing drink made with turmeric, milk, and a touch of sweetness.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup milk
  • ½ tsp turmeric powder
  • Pinch of black pepper
  • 1 tsp honey or jaggery

Steps:

  1. Heat milk with turmeric and pepper.
  2. Simmer for 3–5 minutes.
  3. Strain and sweeten with honey.
  4. Serve warm.
Toast + Tea

Simple toasted bread with butter paired with a soothing cup of tea.

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices bread
  • Butter
  • Your preferred tea

Steps:

  1. Toast the bread and spread butter.
  2. Prepare tea as per preference.
  3. Enjoy together warm.
Ghee Topped Rice

Plain rice drizzled with golden ghee – minimal, calming, and delicious.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 1 tbsp ghee
  • Salt to taste

Steps:

  1. Cook rice and plate it.
  2. Drizzle hot ghee over it.
  3. Sprinkle a pinch of salt and serve.
Veggie Daliya

Wholesome broken wheat porridge cooked with colorful vegetables and spices.

Ingredients:

  • ½ cup daliya (broken wheat)
  • Mixed vegetables (carrot, beans, peas)
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp oil/ghee, salt, water

Steps:

  1. Sauté cumin and veggies in oil.
  2. Add daliya and roast lightly.
  3. Add water and salt, cook until soft.
Upma

A savory South Indian breakfast made from semolina and tempered spices.

Ingredients:

  • ½ cup rava (semolina)
  • Mustard seeds, urad dal
  • Curry leaves, green chili
  • Chopped veggies, salt, water

Steps:

  1. Roast rava, keep aside.
  2. Temper spices and veggies.
  3. Add water, bring to boil.
  4. Add rava, cook until fluffy.
Sweet Potato Coconut Curry

Sweet potatoes simmered in a creamy coconut-based gravy with gentle spices.

Ingredients:

  • 2 sweet potatoes (cubed)
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1 tsp curry powder
  • 1 onion, 1 garlic clove
  • Salt, oil

Steps:

  1. Sauté onion and garlic.
  2. Add sweet potatoes and spices.
  3. Pour coconut milk and simmer.
  4. Cook till potatoes are tender.
